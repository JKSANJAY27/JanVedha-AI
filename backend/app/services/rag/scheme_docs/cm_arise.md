# Chief Minister's Adi Dravidar and Tribal Socio-Economic Development Scheme (CM-ARISE)

## Overview
CM-ARISE is an economic development scheme spearheaded by the Tamil Nadu Adi Dravidar Housing and Development Corporation (TAHDCO) to foster entrepreneurship among the Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) populations in Tamil Nadu. The scheme facilitates self-employment by providing substantial credit-linked subsidies for viable business projects.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Community:** Applicants must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Caste (Arunthathiyar) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) communities.
- **Age:** The entrepreneur's age should be between 18 and 65 years.
- **Income limit:** The family's annual income must not exceed ₹3,00,000 (Three Lakh Rupees).
- **Previous Benefits:** Must not have availed of similar massive subsidies under other central/state economic assistance schemes recently.
- **Project Scope:** The proposed project can be in agriculture-allied sectors, manufacturing, trades, services, or transportation (e.g., buying a commercial vehicle, setting up a clinic, retail shop, dairy farming).
- **Skill Requirements:** Certain projects (like clinics, IT ventures) require the applicant to have corresponding educational or skill qualifications.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card
- Community Certificate
- Income Certificate
- Ration Card
- Quotation or Project Report from authorized suppliers/agencies detailing the project cost.
- Educational/Skill certificates (if the project requires them).
- Driving License and Badge (if applying for commercial vehicles).

## Application Process
1. Online application via the TAHDCO web portal (`application.tahdco.com`).
2. Selecting the specific trade/business activity.
3. Uploading all required personal, financial, and project documents.
4. The application is reviewed by the District TAHDCO task force, and upon approval, is forwarded to a Bank for loan sanction.
5. Subsidy is released as front-end assistance directly to the bank account upon loan approval.

## Contact / Portal
- **State Portal:** http://application.tahdco.com / tahdco.tn.gov.in
- **Local Contact:** TAHDCO District Manager at the respective District Collectorate.

## Financial Benefit
- The scheme provides up to ₹10 Lakhs as a term loan.
- Includes a 35% interest subsidy/capital subsidy component to substantially reduce the burden on the entrepreneur.
