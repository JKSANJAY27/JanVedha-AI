# Tamil Nadu Social Security Pensions (Old Age, Widow, Disabled)

## Overview
The Government of Tamil Nadu administers several social security pension schemes to protect the most vulnerable populations who are unable to earn a livelihood due to age, widowhood, or disability. The primary schemes include the Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS), Destitute Widow Pension Scheme, and the Differently Abled Pension Scheme.

## Eligibility Criteria

### 1. Old Age Pension (OAP)
- **Age:** Must be 60 years or above.
- **Economic Status:** Must be destitute (without any regular income or financial support from family).
- **Ownership:** Must not own property worth more than ₹1,00,000.
- **Residency:** Must be a resident of Tamil Nadu.

### 2. Destitute Widow Pension Scheme
- **Marital Status:** Must be a widow.
- **Age:** Must be 18 years or above.
- **Economic Status:** Must be destitute with no source of income and no adult son capable of supporting her.
- **Limitations:** Remarriage disqualifies the beneficiary from this pension.

### 3. Differently Abled Pension Scheme
- **Disability:** Must have a disability percentage of 40% or more.
- **Age:** Must be 18 years or above (children are covered under separate maintenance grants).
- **Economic Status:** The destitute and income limits are more relaxed for differently-abled individuals compared to OAP. Beneficiaries receiving this pension DO NOT disqualify their families from receiving the Kalaignar Magalir Urimai Thogai.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card
- Smart Ration Card
- Age verification proof / Death certificate of spouse (for Widow Pension)
- National Identity Card for Differently Abled (UDID) / Medical Board Certificate (for Disability Pension).
- Bank Passbook for Aadhaar-seeded accounts.

## Application Process
1. Obtain the application form from the Taluk Office or e-Sevai Centre.
2. Submit the filled form with required enclosures to the local e-Sevai centre.
3. Applications are forwarded to the Revenue Inspector (RI) / Village Administrative Officer (VAO) for field verification.
4. Special camps or regular processing by the Tahsildar (Social Security Scheme) finalizes the approval.

## Contact / Portal
- **State Portal:** tnesevai.tn.gov.in
- **Local Contact:** Revenue Village Administrative Officer (VAO) / Taluk Office (Tahsildar - Social Security).

## Financial Benefit
- The base monthly pension for destitutes, widows, and unmarried women is ₹1,200 per month.
- Differently-abled individuals receive a pension of ₹1,500 per month.
- Benefits are credited directly to the beneficiary's bank account via DBT every month.
