# Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) 2.0

## Overview
PMUY 2.0 is a scheme by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MOPNG) aimed at providing clean cooking fuel (LPG connections) to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households, safeguarding their health from the hazards of burning fossil fuels like firewood and cow dung.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Applicant Profile:** Output applicant must be a woman aged 18 years and above.
- **Household Restriction:** There should not be any other LPG connection from any OMC (Oil Marketing Company) in the same household.
- **Target Categories:** The applicant must belong to one of the following categories:
  - SC Households
  - ST Households
  - Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) Beneficiaries
  - Most Backward Classes (MBC)
  - Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) beneficiaries
  - Tea and Ex-Tea Garden tribes
  - Forest Dwellers
  - People residing in Islands and River Islands
  - SECC Households (AHL TIN)
  - Poor Household as per a 14-point declaration.
- Migrant workers are exempt from providing local address proof and can apply via a self-declaration.

## Required Documents
- e-KYC (Mandatory).
- Aadhaar Card of the applicant as Proof of Identity and Proof of Address.
- Ration Card issued by the State indicating the family composition OR Self-Declaration for migrant applicants.
- Bank Account Number and IFSC Code.
- Supplementary KYC documents if Aadhaar address does not match current address.

## Application Process
1. Online application via the PMUY portal (`pmuy.gov.in`).
2. Offline application: The applicant can visit the nearest LPG distributor (Indane, Bharat Gas, HP Gas) and submit the filled application form along with the required documents.

## Contact / Portal
- **Central Portal:** https://www.pmuy.gov.in
- **Ujjwala Helpline:** 1800-266-6696
- **Local Contact:** Nearest LPG Gas Distributor agency.

## Financial Benefit
- The Government of India provides financial assistance of ₹1600 per connection (which covers security deposit for a 14.2 kg cylinder, pressure regulator, LPG hose, domestic gas consumer card, and inspection/installation charges).
- PMUY 2.0 also provides the first LPG refill and a hotplate (stove) completely free of cost to the beneficiary.
