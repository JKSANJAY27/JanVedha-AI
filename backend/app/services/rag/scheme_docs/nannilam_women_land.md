# Nannilam Women Landownership Scheme

## Overview
The Nannilam Women Landownership Scheme is a specific welfare scheme launched in 2024 by the Tamil Nadu government. It specifically empowers landless Dalit (Scheduled Caste) and Adivasi (Scheduled Tribe) women by providing financial assistance to purchase agricultural land in their own names.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Gender & Category:** The applicant must be a woman belonging to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) community.
- **Land Ownership:** The applicant and her family must be completely landless agricultural laborers.
- **Income Limit:** The annual family income of the applicant should not exceed ₹3,00,000 (Three Lakh Rupees).
- **Age Limit:** Generally between 18 and 65 years.
- **Occupation:** The applicant should primarily be engaged in agricultural labor.

## Required Documents
- Community Certificate (SC/ST)
- Income Certificate
- Aadhaar Card
- Smart Ration Card
- Certificate from the Village Administrative Officer (VAO) verifying that the applicant's family is completely landless.
- Documents pertaining to the land intended to be purchased (chitta, adangal, FMB sketch, parent documents).
- Agreement of sale from the seller.

## Application Process
1. Applications can be submitted online through the TAHDCO (Tamil Nadu Adi Dravidar Housing and Development Corporation) portal.
2. Formally fill out the application and upload necessary SC/ST, Income, and VAO (landless) certificates.
3. Submit the details of the specific land parcel intended to be purchased.
4. The District Level Committee screens and approves eligible applications.

## Contact / Portal
- **State Portal:** http://application.tahdco.com
- **Local Contact:** District TAHDCO Manager Office / District Collectorate.

## Financial Benefit
- The scheme provides financial assistance up to a certain significant percentage of the land cost, enabling SC/ST women to become property owners, thereby fostering long-term economic independence and social empowerment.
