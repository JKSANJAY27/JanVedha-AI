# Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) 2.0

## Overview
PMAY-U 2.0 is a flagship housing scheme launched by the Government of India to provide all-weather pucca houses to all eligible urban households. The 2.0 version extends the scheme to cover an additional 1 crore urban poor and middle-class families over five years (2024-2029). 

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Family Definition:** A beneficiary family comprises a husband, wife, and unmarried children.
- **Housing Status:** The beneficiary family must NOT own a pucca house in their name anywhere in India.
- **Income Criteria:**
  - Economically Weaker Section (EWS): Annual household income up to ₹3,000,000 (₹3 Lakh).
  - Low Income Group (LIG): Annual household income from ₹3,000,001 to ₹6,000,000 (₹3-6 Lakh).
  - Middle Income Group (MIG): Annual household income from ₹6,000,001 to ₹9,000,000 (₹6-9 Lakh).
- **Previous Benefits:** The family must not have availed of any central assistance under earlier housing schemes from the Government of India or under PMAY-U 1.0.
- Mandatory Aadhaar Card for all eligible members.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card (mandatory for identity)
- Income Certificate or Self-declaration of Income
- Proof of Address (Voter ID, Ration Card, etc.)
- Bank Account Details
- Affidavit stating the family does not own a pucca house
- Property/Land documents (if applying for Beneficiary-Led Construction)

## Application Process
Citizens can apply directly via the centralized portal `pmaymis.gov.in` under the "Citizen Assessment" menu, or visit their nearest Urban Local Body (ULB) office or Common Service Centre (CSC) for assisted application submission.

## Contact / Portal
- **Central Portal:** https://pmaymis.gov.in
- **Local Contact:** Nearest Urban Local Body (ULB) / Municipal Corporation Office / Ward Councillor.

## Financial Benefit
- For Economically Weaker Section (EWS): Up to ₹1.5 lakh central assistance. State/ULB share may vary.
- For Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS): Interest subsidy up to 4% on housing loans up to ₹35 lakh.
