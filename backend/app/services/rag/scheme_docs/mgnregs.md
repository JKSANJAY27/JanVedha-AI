# Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS)

## Overview
MGNREGS is a flagship rural livelihood scheme that guarantees 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to a rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. The Act aims to enhance livelihood security in rural areas by providing at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Residency:** Must be a citizen of India residing in a rural area (Gram Panchayat jurisdiction).
- **Age limit:** The applicant must be at least 18 years of age.
- **Willingness:** Must be willing to do unskilled manual work.
- **Household Cap:** Entitlement of 100 days of employment applies per household, not per individual. Multiple adult members can share the 100 days.
- Local Gram Panchayat registered residents only.

## Required Documents
- Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Ration Card)
- Photograph of the applicant
- Bank/Post Office Account Details for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

## Application Process
1. **Registration:** An adult member of a rural household applies for registration to the local Gram Panchayat orally or in writing.
2. **Verification & Job Card:** After verification, the Gram Panchayat registers the household and issues a "Job Card". The Job Card contains details of registered adult members of the household and their photographs.
3. **Demand for Work:** The registered individual demands work in writing (for at least 14 days continuously) from the Gram Panchayat or Program Officer.
4. **Allocation of Work:** Work is to be provided within 15 days of demanding it, failing which an unemployment allowance must be paid. Work should ordinarily be provided within a 5 km radius of the village.

## Contact / Portal
- **Central Portal:** https://nrega.nic.in
- **Local Contact:** Gram Panchayat Office / Panchayat Secretary

## Financial Benefit
- State-specific minimum wage rates governed by the central government. Paid directly into the beneficiary's bank account within 15 days of work completion.
- Unemployment allowance if work is not provided within 15 days of demand.
- Free medical treatment and ex-gratia payment in case of injury or death at the worksite.
